Overall Context:
A stage based policy for a student entrepreneurship program that maps support to team maturity/stage at which they join the program. The policy defines three stages: Idea, Prototype, Building/MVP, the type of support and benefits each stage receives (Mentorship, Financial, Academic and Infra), who is eligible, and the operational rules for attendance waivers, deferment, mentorship, and funding. It is built to make selection, approvals, and resource allocation into a repeatable playbook.
